Detailed Features List

Automatically Show Product Prices Under Product Names in the POS Interface

  • The module displays the sale price of each product directly beneath its name on the POS product grid, improving visibility and reducing the need for extra clicks.

Shop-Level Configuration to Enable or Disable Product Price Display

  • Easily control the visibility of product prices in the POS interface at the shop level. Through POS configuration settings, you can enable or disable the product price display feature for each POS session.

Display Prices for All Product Categories and Variants

  • The price display works across all types of products − including variants, combo products and ensures uniformity across the POS interface.

Make Product Pricing More Visible and Accessible During Sales Transactions

  • Designed with usability in mind: cashiers and customers can instantly see product prices during order taking, which helps speed up the checkout process and reduce errors.
